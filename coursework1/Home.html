On a beautiful Greek island, a group of family and friends come together and sing an array of ABBA songs. Based on a young women about to get married when she discovers that she has three men that could be her father. The bride invites all three to her wedding without her mother Donna Sheridan (Meryl Streep) knowing, hoping her real father will walk her down the isle.
